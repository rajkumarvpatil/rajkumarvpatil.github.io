I'M A EDUCATIONALIST

(Professor)

Prof. Rajkumar Patil is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Technology at MIT-ADT University. With a passion for teaching, he specializes in subjects like machine learning, statistical programming, algorithms, Android programming, and computer vision. His teaching philosophy emphasizes not only imparting knowledge but also fostering a deep understanding and practical application of the subject matter. Alongside his teaching duties, he maintains a strong research portfolio, contributing valuable insights to the field. Prof. Patil is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of IT professionals through his expertise and mentorship.
